Activity 3: Testing the Sports Hall Booking Portal
Log in to your ServiceNow Personal Developer Instance (PDI).
Copy your ServiceNow instance URL.
Example:
          https://yourinstance.service-now.com

Open a new browser tab and access the Service Portal by appending the portal URL suffix (sz) to your instance URL.

Example:
          https://yourinstance.service-now.com/sz

The Sports Zone Service Portal homepage is displayed.
Click Book Venue or Explore Venues.
Select the desired sports facility (e.g., Basketball, Badminton, Table Tennis, etc.).
Choose the booking date and available time slot.
Enter the required booking details and click Proceed.
You will be redirected to the Payment page.
Select the preferred payment method, complete the payment, and click Submit.
The system validates the booking and checks for slot availability.
If the booking is successful:
The reservation is stored in the Sports Hall Bookings table.
The booking status is updated.
A confirmation email containing the venue details, booking date, time slot, and payment information is automatically sent to the user.
If enabled, a scheduled reminder email is sent before the booking start time.

Users can later visit the My Bookings page to view, manage, or cancel their reservations. If a booking is cancelled, the Business Rule automatically deducts the 10% cancellation charge and updates the Refund Payment amount.
